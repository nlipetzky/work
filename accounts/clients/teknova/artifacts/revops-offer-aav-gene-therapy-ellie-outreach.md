# Offer: aav-gene-therapy-ellie-outreach

**Client:** Teknova
**Play:** aav-gene-therapy-ellie-outreach
**Date:** 2026-05-06

---

## Headline

A reagent-readiness offer for AAV gene therapy programs heading toward IND, packaging Teknova's RUO+ small-batch GMP buffers and Express-Tek audit-ready documentation as a way to compress the 6-month lead time and 8-week paperwork cycle that's currently derailing Phase 1 filings, anchored per-prospect to a fresh signal (funding round, IND filing, capacity hire, or recent conference attendance).

---

## Audience

- **Persona:** Director / VP / Head of Process Development, Manufacturing, or CMC at AAV gene therapy developers (biopharma + CDMOs, 50–2,000 employees, US/Canada, preclinical through Phase II). Secondary: Senior Scientists in Process Dev, Manufacturing Leads, Tech Ops Leads. VPs allowed at small/mid biotech only — not at large biopharma.
- **Context:** owns the RUO-to-GMP transition for vector-touching reagents. Under pressure to file IND or progress through Phase 1 without their reagent supply chain triggering FDA pushback. Often first realizes they have a documentation problem 60–90 days before filing, when there's no time to switch suppliers on a 6-month lead time.
- **Pain we address:** the supplier they used in research can't produce GMP-grade batch records, sterility validation on every lot, or endotoxin documentation. Their incumbent's documented turnaround for that paperwork is 8 weeks. They cannot afford a 4-month IND delay, but the alternative — qualifying a new GMP supplier on a 6-month lead time — is worse.

---

## The offer

- **Outcome:** audit-ready process buffers in Phase-1-appropriate volumes (1–25L) with FDA-grade documentation in days, not months. The prospect avoids the 4-month IND-delay scenario without paying full GMP pricing for early-clinical-scale needs.
- **Mechanism:** RUO+ Pathway (small-batch buffers made on the same facility/equipment as Teknova's GMP production, no 6-month lead time) plus Express-Tek Service (audit-ready buffer documentation at RUO pricing). The outreach itself opens with a 20-minute "reagent readiness check" — a working call with Teknova's technical team to walk a specific program against the GxP Reagent Readiness Checklist (12 most common reagent gaps that derail INDs) and identify what's missing.
- **Inclusions:** 17 standard buffer types validated through Teknova's GMP systems; small-batch (1–25L) formats; same-day batch records, sterility validation, endotoxin and lot-consistency documentation; 6 months of safety stock on standard buffers (zero stockout exposure during clinical phases); GxP Reagent Readiness Checklist as a leave-behind.
- **Pricing or commercial frame:** Express-Tek delivers GMP-level documentation at RUO pricing. RUO+ small batches priced for early-clinical scale. Specific SKU pricing not in source — confirm with Jenn before copy.
