## Headline

Outreach to North American mRNA therapeutics developers — conventional mRNA, self-amplifying RNA (saRNA), circular RNA (circRNA), and mRNA-based vaccines/immunotherapies — about Teknova's RUO-through-GMP custom reagent, buffer, and water supply across the full mRNA production workflow, from plasmid template through IVT, purification, LNP formulation, fill-finish, and storage.

---

## Audience

The persona/role this offer is pitched to. Not a database segment.

- **Persona:** Process Development / Bioprocessing / Manufacturing Sciences (MSAT) and Upstream/Downstream leaders; R&D/science owners of the mRNA platform, LNP/formulation development, IVT/drug substance, and analytical development; plus procurement and supply-chain leads sourcing R&D/GMP raw materials. (Full persona tier list lifted to `revops-icp-titles-mrna-therapeutics.md`.)
- **Context:** Works inside an mRNA/RNA therapeutics developer, an mRNA vaccine developer, a CDMO with a dedicated mRNA/LNP line, an LNP/delivery-technology company running formulation wet labs, an IVT-enzyme/nucleotide producer that also runs process/QC labs, or an academic/translational core — that physically runs mRNA process development or manufacturing in North America.
- **Pain we address:** The mRNA process is reagent- and buffer-intensive at every stage — plasmid prep, IVT, purification/polishing, LNP formulation, fill-finish, and storage — and most of those inputs are not catalog-SKU buys at the grades and scales a program needs. Reagents must move from RUO to GMP grade as the program scales; LNP and clinical tech transfer need WFI-quality water in single-use bag formats; custom formulations on slow vendor turnaround stall program timelines.

---

## The offer

What is being pitched, in substance.

- **Outcome:** A reagent supply partner that covers the full mRNA workflow at the grade and turnaround the program needs — so reagent and buffer supply stops being the gating step as the program scales from discovery into PD, clinical, and commercial manufacture in North America.
- **Mechanism:** ISO 13485-certified manufacturing of off-the-shelf and custom RUO- and GMP-grade reagents, with custom formulation and fast turnaround (RUO+ grade, Express•Tek production). Catalog where catalog fits (biological buffers, salt/sugar solutions, dry powders, WFI-quality water); custom service where customization is the point (GMP buffer formulations, single-use bag water formats, custom solutions).
- **Inclusions (mapped to the mRNA workflow, per playbook §1):**
  - **Upstream / plasmid template:** cloning, expansion, lysis, neutralization, and binding/elution buffers (Teknova publishes a dedicated Plasmid Workflow).
  - **IVT:** Tris, HEPES, and other biological buffers; salts (magnesium chloride, sodium chloride); nucleotide/additive solutions; nuclease-free / PCR-certified and WFI-quality water.
  - **Downstream:** chromatography and TFF/UF-DF buffers; salt and chaotropic solutions; surfactants/detergents for clarification and capture.
  - **Formulation (LNP & fill-finish):** formulation, citrate/phosphate, and sucrose/sugar buffers; WFI-quality water in 1 L–200 L single-use bag formats for clinical-scale tech transfer.
  - **Storage:** PluriFreeze (RUO and GMP) cryopreservation media and storage/stabilization buffers for drug substance and cell banks.
- **Pricing or commercial frame:** Not specified in the playbook. Standard custom-formulation engagement model assumed (scope-to-quote, RUO and GMP grade differentiated, single-use bag formats up to 200 L). Flag for sponsor confirmation before copy ships.

## Why now

Specific triggers and market context that make this play timely.

- **Trigger:** mRNA has broadened past infectious-disease vaccines into a durable therapeutic modality — saRNA, circRNA, and mRNA immuno-oncology programs now run active PD and clinical manufacturing in North America. Every program advancing from discovery into process development, clinical, and commercial manufacture ramps reagent and buffer consumption sharply, and pulls RUO→GMP grade demand as it scales.
- **Window:** Structural rather than cyclical — driven by the modality's expansion into new RNA formats and indications. Closer-in window: the engagement's wind-down end-date is 2026-06-25, so this play's send window inside the engagement is the remaining sprint(s).
- **Context:** The playbook's framing (§1) is explicit that the strongest-fit accounts are organizations that consume process reagents repeatedly and at increasing scale as an mRNA program advances. Custom formulation with fast turnaround is the differentiator — off-the-shelf catalog reagents don't cover most of the mRNA buffer/water footprint at GMP grade.

## Out of scope

Explicit boundaries.

- **Not pitched:** IVT enzymes, nucleotides, or capping reagents (the playbook treats producers of these as potential *buyers* of Teknova buffers/water, not as something Teknova sells — see §2 and the existing-account override in §5). LNP lipids / delivery chemistry (Teknova supplies the formulation/buffer/water inputs, not the lipids). Distribution or resale of third-party reagents.
- **Not promised:** Specific turnaround times beyond what Express•Tek formally commits to. Off-the-shelf availability of custom-formulated GMP buffers. GMP-grade output without the custom-formulation engagement.
- **Not for:** Discovery-only or purely computational/AI RNA-design shops with no wet lab or process-reagent consumption. siRNA/ASO/oligonucleotide-only companies with no messenger-RNA therapeutic program. Organizations whose only modality is unrelated to mRNA (small-molecule-only, antibody-only, AAV/gene-therapy with no RNA program, diagnostics-only). Companies with no production or R&D lab footprint in North America. Distributors, pure research-tool resellers, and CROs with no relevant reagent consumption.
