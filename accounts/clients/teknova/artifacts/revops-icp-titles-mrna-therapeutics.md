## Sole-contact exception (§5.1)

A Tier-4 or off-list title at a strongly-qualified account may still pass if it is the best available contact. A clearly out-of-scope role (facilities, legal, HR) passes only if it is the **sole** contact at a high-value qualified account — and then it is flagged for human review, not auto-included.
