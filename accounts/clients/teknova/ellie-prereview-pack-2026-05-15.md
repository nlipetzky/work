## What the system covers today (honest reach)

The 35 concentrate, by design, in the established AAV gene-therapy families: bleeding disorders, inherited retinal/ocular disease, neuromuscular, rare metabolic/CNS, and emerging cardiac. This is the conservative confirmed core from one source — it holds what it cannot verify from trials alone rather than guess. Production-tool CDMOs, preclinical companies with no registered trials, and programs hidden under a partner's name are exactly what the next sources (patent filings, the ASGCT directory, and the others on the roadmap) are built to add. The confirmed list grows as sources layer in; it does not start broad and get filtered down.

## How this works going forward

We deliver in stages by design. The company layer — *is it genuinely AAV* — is the hard part and it is now provable. The fit layer — *is it the right size and stage for the profile you sell into* — is the next stage, and your "outside our profile" calls here are what build it. Once it is clear we are consistently producing the right companies, the contact, enrichment, and Salesforce-context layers are routine and we scale them fast. Stage one was the engineering problem; stage two is fit and volume.
