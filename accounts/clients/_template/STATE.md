## Hard rules

- **No time-bound commitments outside the Trajectory.** Sprint is the unit. Outputs land at the standing review session of the sprint week, not on arbitrary dates promised in email.
- **Written request gate.** Any new ask from the client requires written request before work begins. Verbal asks get acknowledged with "send that in writing so I can scope it."
- **Scope-change protocol.** Any ask outside the Trajectory returns a Scope-Change Notification within 48 hours with three options (add and re-price, defer, decline).
- **Status update fallback.** If a sprint can't produce its expected output, the deliverable is a status update at the standing session, not a partial deliverable and not a missed sprint.
- **No promises that bypass the sprint cadence.** Don't draft commitments saying "I'll have X by Y" outside the sprint structure. The Trajectory governs.
