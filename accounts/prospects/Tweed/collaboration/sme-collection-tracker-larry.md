  here ... what you did before this, and the one-liner you'd use to introduce yourself in a room."
- **War Stories (#5):** "What's an engagement you're proud of ... where you walked in, found the real
  problem, and it actually changed something? I want the story, not the resume version."

  to be doing?" (Ask only after seat is agreed ... it presumes the partnership.)
