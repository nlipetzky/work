# Learning Questions

The questions early prospect conversations are meant to answer. This artifact evolves: questions get answered, replaced, or added as the market teaches us. Approved learnings from the Learnings table flow into this doc (updating each question's status) and into the downstream artifacts they affect.

## Status notation

- **Still learning** ... no signal yet
- **Partial signal** ... early evidence; not enough confidence to act
- **Answered** ... enough conversations to confidently update downstream artifacts

---

## Q1. Which pain actually exists, and how do buyers name it in their own words?

**Status:** Still learning.
**Why it matters:** The current pain hypothesis (expert bottleneck, MQL-to-SQL leak, weak cross-sell from existing accounts) is informed but not validated. The language buyers volunteer unprompted is the language we lead with going forward.
**Downstream artifacts updated when answered:** Pain-to-Cluster Map, Pitch Sheet, Discovery Question Library.

## Q2. Does the Diagnostic close cold, or do prospects need a sample?

**Status:** Still learning.
**Why it matters:** The whole motion rests on the Diagnostic being a buyable, productized deliverable. If prospects respond with "send the contract" we're set. If they ask "can I see a sample of what the Diagnostic produces?" we need to add a redacted sample to the offer package.
**Downstream artifacts updated when answered:** Pitch Sheet, Proposal Template, possibly a new Diagnostic Sample artifact.

## Q3. Who actually decides, and what does the buying process look like?

**Status:** Still learning.
**Why it matters:** Right now we're talking to operators. They may be the buyer, the champion, or both. Knowing the typical decision profile lets us multi-thread properly from the first meeting.
**Downstream artifacts updated when answered:** Discovery Question Library, Pitch Sheet (multi-threading guidance).

## Q4. What objections recur?

**Status:** Still learning.
**Why it matters:** After ~20 conversations, the recurring pushbacks form a library. Pre-written answers in-voice make responses faster and more consistent.
**Downstream artifacts updated when answered:** New Objection Library artifact (created when 3+ distinct objections recur).

## Q5. Which Cluster gets pulled at most?

**Status:** Still learning.
**Why it matters:** Real conversations show which existing or emergent Cluster has the strongest demand. That tells us where to sharpen the offer and which Cluster might be the first to stabilize into a Constellation.
**Downstream artifacts updated when answered:** Pain-to-Cluster Map, Pitch Sheet.

## Q6. Does the "assets-per-week" framing land, or do prospects expect hours?

**Status:** Still learning.
**Why it matters:** The productization rests on prospects accepting that the deliverable is assets shipped, not consulting hours. If they default to expecting hours, the framing needs sharper language in every pitch.
**Downstream artifacts updated when answered:** Pitch Sheet, Delivery Models.

## Q7. What price posture lands?

**Status:** Still learning.
**Why it matters:** Diagnostic pricing and retainer tier pricing are best tested in market. Flinches and "send me the price" timing tell us where the curve sits.
**Downstream artifacts updated when answered:** Proposal Template; future Pricing doc.

## How this loop runs

1. Conversation happens. Notes captured on the Prospect record and the related Event in the CRM.
2. After each conversation (or in synthesis every 5-10), individual insights get logged in the Learnings table.
3. Each Learning is reviewed: Approved, Rejected, or Superseded.
4. Approved learnings update this doc (the relevant question's status and any context) AND the downstream artifact named on the Learning record.
5. When a question reaches "Answered," it retires from active learning. New questions are added as they emerge.

## Notes on this loop

- The questions above are a starter set. Expect them to be revised after the first 5 conversations.
- "Answered" doesn't mean closed forever ... revisit when the offer shape changes or when a new Cluster enters the catalog.
- Questions that turn out to be wrong (asking about the wrong thing) get Superseded by better-framed questions, not just answered.
