**When this lands:** founder-to-founder DMs, voice memos, internal Slack, sales calls with peer-level operators. **When it does not land:** anything client-facing, anything legal, anything that goes to a regulated buyer or a public-company board. Voice in copy should preserve the bluntness and rhythm WITHOUT the profanity unless the audience explicitly warrants it.
