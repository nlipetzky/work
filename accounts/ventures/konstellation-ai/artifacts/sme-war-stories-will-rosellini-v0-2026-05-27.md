### Story: perimeter-medical-breast-cancer-tool
- Setup: Perimeter Medical built an AI tool to reduce breast cancer resection redo surgeries.
- Conflict: They needed a benchmark to validate the tool against. The surgeons whose performance was worst (35% redo rate) had no documented records. The academics with documented records were already at 2-5%.
- What Will did: Watched the validation problem destroy commercial uptake.
- Outcome: The people who need the tool refuse to expose their performance to evaluate it. Top achievers don't need it. No middle market.
- Lesson: In regulated professional environments, validation runs into a Catch-22 ... the people who need the tool the most won't document their failures, so you can't prove the tool works on them.
