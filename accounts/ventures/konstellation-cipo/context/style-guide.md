## Hard constraints encoded (do not violate)

- **No astronomical / space / "constellation" motifs**, naming, gradients, or
  imagery. CIPO cannot inherit KAI's astronomical frame (Will's controlled-lexicon
  refusal; "do not auto-inherit"). `[src: controlled-lexicon, voice-codex]`

  advisor," not "legal SaaS," not "space."
- **Single conservative accent.** No AI purple/pink gradients.
- Banned vocabulary never appears in chrome or copy
