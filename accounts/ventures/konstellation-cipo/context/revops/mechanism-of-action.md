# Mechanism of Action — curated source material

Provenance: Will's "IP Intelligence Platform" doc (2026-05-19, adapted to
Konstellation) + 2026-06-23 transcript.

## How the value is produced (input → outcome, causal steps)
1. **Client onboards** to a subscription tier (or buys credits) and names its
   technology space and competitors.
2. **Agentic intelligence layer runs.** ~20 autonomous, methodology-driven workflows
   execute structured IP analysis on demand: FTO, patentability, prior-art,
   invalidity/validity, claim mapping, landscapes, portfolio strategy, valuation,
   licensing, competitor monitoring, trade-secret ID. Each follows a documented SOP,
   cites sources, and classifies findings with standardized frameworks — producing
   deliverables in hours that traditionally take days/weeks.
3. **Continuous monitoring** tracks competitor filings, publications, grants, and
   regulatory submissions; clients get monthly digests with escalation alerts.
4. **CIPO advisory overlay.** Will (CIPO) interprets the agentic output into
   decisions: board guidance, investor prep, prosecution strategy. The AI produces
   data; the CIPO produces judgment.
5. **IP Velocity Score** composes the portfolio's strength/momentum/positioning into
   one monthly metric (depth, competitive position, prosecution velocity, commercial
   alignment, risk), reviewed on advisory calls — making progress visible and
   investor-presentable.

## Why it holds up under pressure (the rigor)
The workflows are structured methodology engines, not open-ended prompts; every
deliverable carries documented search parameters, source citations, the 18-month
publication-lag disclaimer, and CIPO review before client delivery. The agentic
layer is an analytical engine, not a replacement for professional judgment.
