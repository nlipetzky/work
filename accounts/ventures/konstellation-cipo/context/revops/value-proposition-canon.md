## The value (what, to whom, why defensible)
Konstellation gives startups continuous, AI-powered IP intelligence plus a human
Chief IP Officer's judgment, at startup-accessible subscription pricing, replacing
unpredictable hourly IP billing. The line that captures it: the AI does the
analysis, the CIPO provides the judgment, the client gets both.

To whom: early- and growth-stage startups (pre-seed through Series A/B) that need a
real IP position for fundraising and defensibility but are priced out of traditional
IP consulting.

Why defensible: a stack of ~20 methodology-driven agentic IP workflows + proprietary
SOPs + Will Rosellini's domain expertise as CIPO. Competitors can buy AI tools; they
cannot replicate the integrated methodology. Continuous monitoring and the IP
Velocity Score create ongoing value the incumbents' episodic, project-based model
cannot.

## Comparable / template
