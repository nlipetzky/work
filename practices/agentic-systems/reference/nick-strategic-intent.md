## What "the system is the goal" means here

Time inside an Area can be in one of two modes:

- **Build mode** — constructing the agentic system that delivers value in that Area
- **Operate mode** — orchestrating the system, reviewing output, making decisions

Both count toward the Area's allocation. The healthy ratio shifts toward Operate as systems mature.

**Teknova RevOps lifecycle (rough):**

- Now: mid-early build. ~80% build, 20% operate.
- 4–6 weeks: mid-mature. ~40% build, 60% operate.
- 3+ months: mature. ~20% build, 80% operate. Productization complete.

Do not endlessly build. Each system needs a v1 convergence point where it runs and produces output.

## Areas and what should be systematized

- **Client engagement (Teknova):** systematize. The RevOps system is the deliverable.
- **Prospect engagement (queued prospects, partner, Nick's own pipeline):** systematize. Same RevOps system, different ICPs loaded.
- **Infrastructure (Staff, AI exploration):** systematize selectively. Build only what supports current operating reality. Friday tinkering is the sanctioned outlet.
- **Finance:** systematize the recurring (accounting routine). Don't systematize one-off cleanup.
- **Admin:** systematize the recurring (hiring pipeline via Upwork). Don't systematize one-offs.
- **Personal:** do NOT systematize. Presence-deriving work. Family time, calls home, time-sensitive personal admin (Edgewood comps, mom rental).
